Snoopy Presents!!

Snoopy woke early and stretched on top of his red doghouse. The air was cool and quiet and he watched the street slowly come alive. A cold root beer sat beside him and it felt like the perfect start to the day.

He had promised himself that today would be for writing. The typewriter was ready, the page was blank, and Woodstock stayed close by bringing snacks. Snoopy tapped a few words then leaned back, eyes closed, resting in the sun while the story waited.

The novel can wait. Some days are for small joys and easy moments. Later there will be dancing in the yard and a visit with Charlie Brown. For now the quiet feels enough.
